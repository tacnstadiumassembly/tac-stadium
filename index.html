Welcome to The Apostolic Church Nigeria (TACN)

The Apostolic Church Nigeria is a Bible-believing church dedicated to raising spiritually grounded believers through sound doctrine, prayer, worship, and love. We exist to preach the gospel of Jesus Christ and build a community of faith where lives are transformed.

Our Ministries

Worship Ministry

Leading the church into God's presence through praise, worship, and music.

Youth Ministry

Raising a generation of young believers rooted in faith, purpose, and discipline.

Women Ministry

Empowering women to grow spiritually and live Christ-centered lives.

Men Ministry

Building godly men with strong leadership, faith, and integrity.
