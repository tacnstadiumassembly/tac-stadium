The Apostolic Church Nigeria is a Bible-believing, Holy Spirit-filled church dedicated to raising spiritually grounded believers through sound doctrine, fervent prayer, heartfelt worship, and genuine love. Founded on the principles of the early apostolic church, we exist to proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ and build a community of faith where lives are transformed by the power of God.

At Stadium Assembly, you'll find a warm family ready to welcome you, support you in your walk with Christ, and grow together in the grace and knowledge of our Lord Jesus.
