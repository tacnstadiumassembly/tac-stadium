Giving & Donations

Honoring God through faithful stewardship.

The Heart of Giving

Giving is an act of worship and trust in God. When we give, we acknowledge that everything we have comes from Him and belongs to Him. Your generous support enables this church to carry out its mission — providing ministry, pastoral care, community outreach, and support for those in need.

Whether through tithes, offerings, or special donations, every gift makes a difference. Thank you for partnering with us in advancing the Kingdom of God.

Ways to Give

There are several ways you can support the work of God through our church.

Tithes

The tithe is the first 10% of your income, given faithfully to support God's house. It is an act of obedience and trust in God's provision.

Offerings

Offerings are gifts given cheerfully beyond your tithe. They express gratitude to God and support various church activities and programs.

Missions

Support our mission efforts locally and globally. Your gifts help spread the Gospel, support missionaries, and reach communities in need.

Special Projects

Contribute to specific church projects such as building renovations, equipment, community programs, and special events.
