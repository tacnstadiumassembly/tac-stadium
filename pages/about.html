Who We Are

Stadium Assembly is part of The Apostolic Church Nigeria, a fellowship rooted in Scripture, prayer, and Christian community. We've been serving the Ogbomosho community for many years, and our mission remains the same: to share the love of Jesus and help people grow in their faith.

Our Mission

We exist to proclaim the Word of God faithfully, raise disciples grounded in truth, and serve our community with love and compassion.

Our Vision

We dream of a church where every person — young or old, new or experienced — feels at home and is equipped to live out their faith in everyday life.

What We Believe

  • The Bible is God's inspired Word and our guide for life
  • Salvation comes through faith in Jesus Christ alone
  • Prayer is powerful and changes things
  • The Holy Spirit is active and present today
  • We are called to love God and love people

Our Leadership

Stadium Assembly is led by G. Pastor Bode, under the oversight of The Apostolic Church Nigeria. Our leadership is committed to teaching the Bible clearly, caring for the congregation, and building a church that honours God.
