We Can't Wait to Meet You

Visiting a new church can feel a little uncertain. You might be wondering:

  • What should I wear?
  • Where do I park?
  • Will I feel out of place?
  • What about my children?

We want to answer those questions so you can walk in feeling comfortable and confident.

When We Meet

  • Sunday School: 8:00 AM – 9:00 AM (or up to 9:30 AM)
  • Main Service: Immediately after Sunday School
  • Services usually end: Around 12:00 PM, as led by the Spirit

What to Expect

Our services include:

  • Worship through songs and hymns
  • Prayer together as a congregation
  • A message from the Bible (usually 30–45 minutes)
  • A warm, respectful atmosphere

We're not loud or chaotic. We're not stiff or formal either. Just genuine people gathering to worship God.

What to Wear

There's no dress code. Some people wear traditional attire, some wear casual clothes. Wear whatever makes you feel comfortable and ready to worship.

Parking and Arrival

We don't have a private parking lot, but street parking is available nearby. We recommend arriving 10–15 minutes early so you can find a seat and settle in.

Children and Youth

Yes! We have Sunday School classes for children and young people. Your kids will be in a safe, caring environment where they can learn about God at their level.
